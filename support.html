How You Can Support Us

There are many ways to support Worcester Deaf Children's Society - whether you donate, fundraise, sponsor or simply spread the word, every action helps Deaf children and their families feel supported and included.

Make a Donation

Your support helps us fund family events, BSL courses, interpreter support and resources for Deaf children and their families. Whether it's a one-off or regular donation it all makes a difference. Did you know you can set up a monthly Direct Debit?

Alternatively, you could donate in the form of goods or services such as catering or refreshements at our events, raffle prizes to raise funds, event equipment or storage.

Offer a Venue

If you run a space that could host one of our events, please get in touch. Free or low cost venues allow us to spend more of our funds directly supporting families.

Fundraise for Us

Whether it's running a marathon or park run, organising a bake sale... and we'll be there every step of the way with tips and guidance. You can set up your fundraising page over here on JustGiving.

Sponsorship

Partner with us to make a lasting difference for Deaf children and their families whilst supporting your local community.

Contact us to find out more about sponsorship opportunities.
