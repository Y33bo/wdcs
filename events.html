Events

Join us at our Pool Party from 3:30pm (3:15pm arrival) on Sunday 19th April 2026.

We have booked the full pool from 3:30pm - 4:15pm and then the side room 4:30pm - 5pm for refreshments after our party.

Please arrive at 3.15pm to allow time to change. The pool is a heated pool - half of the pool will have a small inflatable obstacle course (deep end) and the other half of the pool will have floats and balls to play with (shallow end).

Lifeguards are on hand and floating vests are available for non swimmers to access the inflatable, adults are not allowed on the inflatable but can be in the water next to it.

This activity is from ages 0+ so open to all - Children, siblings and parents!

Tickets are £10 per family (however if this is a barrier to your attendance please do email us separately).

We look forward to seeing you there!
