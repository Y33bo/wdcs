Welcome to the Worcester Deaf Children's Society

We are a community for Deaf, Hard of Hearing, DeafBlind and CODA children and their families.

We are a local voluntary group run by a committee for families of Deaf children.

We provide monthly activities, it's free to join and most of our events are free to attend.

Join us for fun-filled events, community and useful resources.
